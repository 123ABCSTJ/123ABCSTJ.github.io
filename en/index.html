Our Mission

is to provide high quality service to children and parents in an intimate environment.

Welcome

At 123 ABC we offer private child care services in English and French by a trained educator who has the overall development of child at heart. We follow the curriculum set in Quebec’s Educational Program for Childcare Services "Meeting Early Childhood Needs"

Our major goal is to create a healthy, stimulating and creative setting, for the better development of each child so that they may achieve their full potential. The day's activities allow children to learn and develop cooperation, autonomy and mutual aid. Our activities are designated to meet the needs of children by stimulating all areas of development: physiological, physical and fine motor, social, intellectual, language, emotional all within a safe environment.

$29 (2-5 years) or 34$ (6-24 months) per day for a child full time, $35 per day for part-time (less than 5 days of the week). This includes two health snacks and a highly nutritious meal for lunch, following Canada's Food Guide 

Cost is tax deductible and eligible for refundable tax credit for child care expenses tax.

We are located on André-Prévost in Saint-Jerome.
We can accommodate up to 6 children between the ages 6 months to 5 years.
Opening hours are Monday to Friday 7h30 - 17h30.
